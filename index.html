My UX Skills

USER RESEARCH

I approach User Research in a comprehensive way. When I am faced with a new issue I want to know everything I can about it and how it is being addressed.

USER EMPATHY

User empathy is the space where I feel the most comfortable. Once I know who the user is, it is very easy for me to imagine the circumstances they find themselves in.

I enjoy qualitative interviews and I am always surprised by the new things that I learn when I interview people. It expands my world view and reminds me not to make assumptions.
